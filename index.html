TMAC's Mission

  • Nurture our cultural heritage, values to our generations and appreciate other cultures
  • Assist new immigrants to settle, community members to thrive, youth to become leaders
  • Encourage a supportive community to benefit society through volunteering and charity activities
  • Be an advocate of active healthy life style through sports, festival celebrations, and cultural gatherings
  • Enrich ourselves, our origin and be a valuable member of Canadian multiculturalism
